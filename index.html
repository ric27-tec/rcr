Making sense of life Through movement, change, work, family, and the pursuit of happiness, even in chaos.

I've lived across countries, built things, changed paths, lost, started over, and kept trying to make sense of an ever-changing world. I've become a collector of experiences, drawn to learning, creating, and seeing life from different angles.


This space brings together reflections on personal growth, society, creativity, technology, art, memory, and change. Not to find perfect answers, but to keep questioning, creating, and paying closer attention to what makes life worth living in the brief moment we have.
